📜 Usage Guide

🤖 How to Use the Bot

  • 📴 Put your phone in Airplane Mode for 2–3 minutes
  • 🧹 Clear Wolt App Data (see below)
  1. Open the bot in Telegram
  2. Tap 🍖 Get Wolt Account
  3. Open the Wolt app and log in using the provided email
  4. Wait for 🩵 Complete Login button and tap it
📡 Important: All orders must be made using mobile internet (4G/5G).
Using Wi-Fi may cause loss of discount or account ban.

Choose your device to view instructions

🧹 How to Clear Wolt App Data

⚠️ This will completely erase:
– all app data,
– login sessions,
– settings,
– cache.
The app will behave like freshly installed.

  1. Enable Airplane Mode
  2. Open Settings on your phone
  3. Go to Apps or Apps & Notifications
  4. Find Wolt app and tap it
  5. Select Storage & cache
  6. Tap Clear storage or Clear data and confirm
  7. Disable Airplane Mode

🧹 How to Remove Wolt App on iPhone

⚠️ This will delete:
– all user data,
– login sessions,
– app settings,
– cache.

The app will be clean after reinstalling.

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Go to General → iPhone Storage
  3. Find and tap Wolt
  4. Tap Delete App
  5. Confirm deletion
  6. Reinstall from the App Store

Note: App must be removed before each new login via WoltBot.
